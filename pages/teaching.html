TEACHING

  • Meditation:
    When visiting Nepal, it is deeply meaningful to take advantage of this sacred journey by receiving authentic meditation teachings, participating in blessing ceremonies, and cultivating inner awareness. You will stay in a peaceful environment near the stupa, offering ideal practice facilities and the precious presence of a realized master.
  • Dzogpa Chenpo:
    The Tulku is a highly accomplished master of the Nyingma School of Tibetan Buddhism, fully versed in Dzogpa Chenpo (Dzogchen), particularly the Longchen Nyingthik lineage—considered the most profound and advanced meditation practice in Tibetan Buddhism.
  • Yoga:
    You will have the opportunity to learn and practice the Yoga of the Mind, Guru Yoga, and related contemplative practices in the sacred and powerful locations of your pilgrimage.
  • Teachings and Ceremonies – In Person & Online:
    While in Kathmandu, you may receive teachings and ceremonies directly in person. After returning to your home country, you can remain connected through online teachings and practice sessions—keeping an open window to Boudha and sustaining your spiritual connection until your next visit.
